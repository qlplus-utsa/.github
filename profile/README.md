# Quality of Life Plus at UT San Antonio

Hi everyone! Quality of Life Plus's mission is to **benefit our community** through the design and development of **assistive medical technologies.** 

We connect passionate, inventive **students with opportunities** to **support our disabled community members**. We work for a future in which **anyone can live with the highest quality of life**, regardless of medical limitations.

Below you will find a **list of all of Quality of Life Plus' projects** throughout the years! We hope these showcase the **capabilities of our organization** and the **potential each new project can bring!**

---

## Our Projects

__**Thermal Heating Sleeve**__
- Designed a comfortable and protective sleeve that keeps the lining around our Challenger's leg amputation warm while operating in an extremely cold environment

__**Tranquility Therapeutics**__ - [News Article About the Company](https://www.utsa.edu/alumni/news/2021/Devanshi%20Patel.html)
- Developed a unique therapeutic breathing device that helps people breathe and overcome hyperventilation while experiencing a panic attack or anxiety and can also aid people who are uncomfortable taking oral medication
- This project has **won UTSA's Venture Technology Competition ($100,000 in funding)**, 2nd place in the Big Rowdy Ideas, and received $1,500 in funding from the Rowdy Tank Sponsorship

__**Recumbent Bike Lift**__ - [News Article About the Team](https://www.expressnews.com/news/education/article/utsa-veterans-disabled-projects-19900688.php)
- Constructed a medical lift that our Challenger can operate independently to get himself into his recumbent bike by extending the control beyond what currently exists for our lift model

__**Corozon Clinic Walker Repair**__
- Repaired a broken walker for Corozon Clinic by modifying the legs, adding new steel, and replacing old wheels to enable safety of patients using the walker

__**TLens**__ - [Project's Documentation](https://github.com/arturo-j-martinez-ii/tlens)
- Developed an open-source transcription device that doesn't require Wi-Fi or a cellular device for a deaf and visually impaired veteran and anyone to use anywhere in the world

__**Sensor Team**__ - [Project’s Documentation](https://github.com/qlplus-utsa/wearablesensor)
- Created a device under $30 to measure and display user’s heartrate and average heartrate with over 95% accuracy
